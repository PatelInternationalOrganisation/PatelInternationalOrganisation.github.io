Param Pujya Naypadmsagarji Maharajsaheb has conceived and conceptualized the process of inspiring to form Patel International Organisation (PIO) for Patel Community as a whole.

Param Pujya Naypadmsagarji Maharajsaheb is the Apex Confederation of all organizations of Patel, spread around the world.

This concept of PIO as an Apex Confederation of all organizations of Patel spread world over and representing different segments of the community, was crystalised by Mr. Dasharathbhai B. Patel after many meetings and deliberations with cross section of the society from different walks of life. It was conceptualized with a view to unite all farmers, businessmen and industrialists of various segments of Patel community. Nearly 75% of the working population of Patel is engaged in agriculture and business. A sizable population engaged in business and services are working in different countries of the World (NRI).

What is PIO

PIO is a collective effort of Patel community all over the world, based on the principle of creating Global awareness of their Life-style, Culture and Heritage. PIO is an unique organization which aims at a giant step to change the world for better. It is a global community where visionaries, intellectuals, risk taking, leadership carrying the people from all walks of life with them under one umbrella. PIO’s basic aim is ‘Complete and total unity of Patels’ all over the world by creating synergy amongst them.

FUTURE INITIATIVES OF PIO

EDUCATION

Establish world class futuristic value based educational systems and institutions to provide opportunities of skill development and continuous skill upgradation.

HEALTH FOR ALL

Provide facilities for better and affordable diagnostic system, medical care, establishment of diagnostic centers, hospitals etc. “A healthy family is a foundation of a happy society”.

ECONOMIC PROSPERITY

Create all round growth through business network and promoting entrepreneurship. Our aim is to provide facilities for business opportunities to the youth, professionals, people engaged in cottage and small scale industries , senior citizens and part-time workers to earn better livelihood.

REAL AND SPEEDY JUSTICE

Establish ‘International Settlement Forum’ (ISF) to facilitate amicable and speedy resolution of all type of disputes at low cost as stipulated under the law.

VISION

PIO intends to bring global harmony, peace and prosperity by creating mutual respect for every living being and strive for a world free of violence, poverty and disease.

MISSION

PIO is committed to building a co-operative environment and social infrastructure for overall growth, development and well-being of the community by improving health, wealth, knowledge and spirituality.

Our aim is to provide fair and optimum opportunities for strong social relationship amongst Patels of different walks of life.

Create a global awareness about our ethos, values and philosophy of our noble and global principles, which will benefit humanity as a whole.

PIO CARD

This card will establish your identity in the society. It will change the way of life with bulk buying benefits combined with technological support which can work as a binding force.

MANY MORE

Create social network, marriage bureau, job portal, spiritual, social and economic growth of individual, family and community as a whole.
